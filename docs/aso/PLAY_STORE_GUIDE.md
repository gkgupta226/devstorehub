# Google Play Store ASO Metadata Guide

Google Play's algorithm functions more like Google Web Search, indexing the entire description.

## 1. App Title (30 Characters Max)
- **Weight**: Highest.
- **Strategy**: Clear, concise, and keyword-rich.

## 2. Short Description (80 Characters Max)
- **Weight**: High.
- **Strategy**: The primary "elevator pitch." Affects conversion and search ranking.

## 3. Long Description (4,000 Characters Max)
- **Weight**: Very High (indexed for search).
- **Strategy**: Repeat your primary keywords 4-5 times naturally throughout the text (Keyword Density). Do not "stuff" keywords.

## 4. Visual Metadata
- **Icon**: Must follow Adaptive Icon guidelines.
- **Feature Graphic**: Vital for "Recommended for You" sections.

## 5. Ratings & Reviews
- Google Play weights review sentiment and keyword presence in reviews higher than Apple.
- Respond to every review to increase your "Response Rate" score.

## 6. Android Vitals
- App stability (low crash rates) directly affects your search visibility on Android.
